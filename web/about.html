What is it?

Faithful 3D is a simple resourcepack for Minecraft. Faithful 3D does not change any default vanilla textures, but replaces many of the default block models. Blocks such as ladders or rails that are a single flat face by default are replaced by one-pixel-thick 3D versions.

How do I use it?

You can download the pack from the Downloads page or Customize page. Once you have downloaded the zip file of the pack, place it in your game's resourcepack folder. In the game, navigate to Settings and then Resource Packs. Once there, you can select Open Pack Folder to open the game's resourcepack folder or, once it's installed, select Faithful 3D from the list of available packs.

Will this pack affect my game's performance?

Yes. By making models 3-dimensional, Faithful 3D adds more faces for the game to render for each block which places more load on your graphics card. This tends to lower the FPS of your game, although it may not be noticable on a higher-end GPU. Customizing your download and removing plentiful blocks like kelp and grass or blocks like redstone that receive a lot of updates can help you get better performance, although at the cost of rendering these blocks in simple 2D.

Is it compatible with X resourcepack?

Probably. Most of the models Faithful 3D uses are designed to properly render any 16x16 texture. Textures of a different resolution may not align to the same pixel grid and may appear strange. A minority of the current models are from and old version of this pack and may not support 16x16 textures that dramatically change the look of a block, but we're working to replace these with updated models!
